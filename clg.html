Savitribai Phule Pune University

The university has various departments and centres, offering courses in science, social science, management, law, etc.

MIT- World Peace University

The university was formerly known as the Maharashtra Institute of Technology, established in 1983 as the first college of the MAEER group of institutions, and was one of the first private engineering colleges in Maharashtra.

Symbiosis International University

Symbiosis International is a multi-campus private, deemed university located in Pune, India. The university has more than 41 academic institutions spread over various campuses in Pune, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Nagpur, Nashik, Noida, etc.

College of Engineering Pune (COEP)

College Of Engineering Pune Technological University is a unitary public university of the Government of Maharashtra, situated in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Established in 1854, it is the 3rd oldest engineering institute in India, after College of Engineering, Guindy and IIT Roorkee.
