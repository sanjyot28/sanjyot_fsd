Transportation in Pune

Pune offers various modes of transportation to navigate the city and beyond. Here's a look at some of the key options:

Metro

Here's the route of Pune metro Line 1 (Pimpri-Chinchwad - Swargate) and Line 2 (Vanaz - Ramwadi) with a combined length of 31.25 km

PMPML

PMPML is a public transportation provider in Pune which operates bus routes. The PMPML has 528 bus routes in Pune with 7890 bus stops.

Pune Airport

Pune Airport is an international airport and an Indian Air Force base serving the city of Pune, Maharashtra, India. It is located at Lohegaon, approximately 11 km northeast of the historic centre of Pune.

Auto Rickshaws

Auto rickshaws are a popular mode of transportation in Pune, known for being affordable, convenient, and easy to find.
